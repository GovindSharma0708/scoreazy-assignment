Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our confidence building program

This microcourse is specifically designed for children in Grades 3-5 (typically ages 8-11), with content and activities appropriate for their developmental stage.

Each week requires about 45-60 minutes of engagement, broken into 15-20 minute sessions. Activities are designed to fit into busy family schedules.

While children can complete most activities independently, parent involvement enhances the experience. We provide guidance for parents on how to support their child's confidence journey.

Students have flexible access to materials and can progress at their own pace. Premium members get extended access to ensure completion without pressure.

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with your child's progress or the course experience.
